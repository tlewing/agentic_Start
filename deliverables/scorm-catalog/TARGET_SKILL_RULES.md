## Course Assignment Rules

### Rule 1: New Hire Requirements (All Roles)
All employees must complete within 30 days of hire:
- One New Hire Safety Orientation course
- What Is Emotional Intelligence?

### Rule 2: Foreman Promotion Requirements
Before promotion to Foreman, must complete:
- Introduction to Field Leadership (all 4 sections)
- Roles and Responsibilities of a Foreman
- LOTO Fundamentals
- Fall Protection basics

### Rule 3: Superintendent Advancement
Before advancement to General Superintendent:
- All Foreman requirements
- Safety Coordinator series (3.0-11.0)
- Critical Leadership Training series
- Full Values Driven Leadership series

### Rule 4: Annual Refresher Requirements
All field personnel must complete annually:
- One safety refresher course
- One leadership/communication course

### Rule 5: Software Training
Role-specific software training required within 60 days of assignment:
- PM: ViewPoint, Accubid overview
- EST: Full Accubid training
- FL/GS: Job Plan Theory

## Proficiency Level Definitions

| Level | Definition | Typical Courses |
|-------|------------|-----------------|
| **Awareness** | Understands concepts and terminology | Intro courses, "What is..." courses |
| **Basic** | Can apply concepts with guidance | Fundamentals, Chapter 1-5 courses |
| **Proficient** | Can apply independently and train others | Full series, Chapter 6-10+ courses |
| **Advanced** | Can handle complex situations, troubleshoot | Advanced courses, complex procedures |
| **Expert** | Mastery level, can develop procedures | All courses + practical experience |
